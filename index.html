RACES

All riders from juniors to professionals compete in criteriums throughout the day on a 0.72 mile tight, technical course through Downtown Shreveport. Races begin with the junior category, culminating in the early evening with the Pro/1/2 men and women racing in pursuit of a $10,000 purse for men and a $10,000 purse for women. Prior to the Pro events, we are hosting a “Kids Ride”, free to children 10 and under. There will be a total of eleven races, by category. See Race Flyer for details

GRAN FONDO

This ride is a community ride, open to all riders, professional, amateur, and recreational. The Gran Fondo is a 100 mile scenic ride beginning in downtown Shreveport, covering a winding route north of the City through some of the most beautiful and wooded areas in Louisiana.

FEIST-WEILLER

The Feist-Weiller Cancer Center (FWCC), designated a Center of Excellence, a leader in cancer research and cancer treatment, is the Regional Referral Center for North Louisiana. As part of a “safety net” hospital system we service a rural core of over 40 sites where screenings and treatment may be unaffordable or unavailable. FWCC serves the insured, the underinsured, and the uninsured.
